### Master Your Consumption, Manage Your Bills

**Lesstrik** is a smart utility companion built with SwiftUI, designed to help users take full control of their monthly electricity expenses. By providing accurate cost calculations based on local tariffs and intuitive data visualizations, Lesstrik empowers users to be more mindful of their energy consumption and avoid unexpected billing surprises.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

* **Instant Bill Estimation**: Calculate your projected monthly electricity bill based on real-time usage and local energy tariffs.
* **Usage Logging**: Easily record and track your meter readings or appliance usage history in a structured manner.
* **Data Visualization**: Interactive charts and graphs that visualize your consumption patterns, helping you identify peak usage periods.
* **Cost Awareness**: Stay informed about how much each unit costs, making it easier to adjust habits and save money.
* **Native SwiftUI Experience**: A fluid, modern, and accessible user interface built entirely with Apple's latest UI framework.

## üß† How It Works
1. **Input Data**: Users log their current meter readings or specific appliance wattages.
2. **Calculate**: The app applies local electricity tariff formulas to the consumption data.
3. **Visualize**: Using **Swift Charts**, the app breaks down the data into easy-to-read visual reports, showing trends over days, weeks, or months.
